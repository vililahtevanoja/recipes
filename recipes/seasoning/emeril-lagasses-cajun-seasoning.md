# Emeril Lagasses Cajun Spice Mix

## Ingredients

- `2.5 tbsp` paprika
- `2 tbsp` sea salt
- `2 tbsp` garlic powder
- `1 tbsp` black pepper
- `1 tbsp` onion powder
- `1 tbsp` cayenne pepper
- `1 tbsp` dried leaf oregano
- `1 tbsp` dried thyme

## Instructions

1. Mix it all together
1. Store in sealable container
