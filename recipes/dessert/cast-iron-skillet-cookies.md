# Easy Cast-Iron Skillet Cookies

## Ingredients

- `240 g` butter
- `180 g` (`2.4 dl`) brown sugar
- `200 g` (`2.4 dl`) white sugar
- `2` eggs
- `1 tsp` vanilla
- `0.75 tsp` salt
- `1 tsp` baking soda
- `240 g` (`4.8 dl`) flour
- `170 g` chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a cast iron skillet.
1. Turn off the heat, add the remaining ingredients, and mix.
1. Bake at `160°C` for 25 minutes. Should be 82-85°C at the center.
